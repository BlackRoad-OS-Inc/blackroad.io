What is BlackRoad OS?

BlackRoad OS is a sovereign AI operating system that runs entirely on your hardware. Built on a fleet of 5 Raspberry Pi nodes with 52 TOPS of Hailo-8 neural compute, it provides persistent AI memory, local inference, and a complete suite of tools for education, content creation, and knowledge work — all without sending your data to the cloud.

Unlike ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, BlackRoad OS remembers every conversation across sessions. Your AI tutor knows what your child studied last week. Your code assistant remembers your project architecture. Your search engine understands your context. This cross-session memory is powered by a local SQLite ledger and Qdrant vector database — no cloud required.

How BlackRoad OS Works

Persistent Memory. Every conversation, preference, and project detail is stored locally. When you come back tomorrow, your AI knows exactly where you left off. No re-explaining. Powered by SQLite + Qdrant on your own hardware.

Local Inference. AI models run on Ollama across your Pi fleet. Llama 3, Phi-3, TinyLlama — all local. No API keys, no rate limits, no monthly token caps. Works offline. 52 TOPS of neural compute via Hailo-8 accelerators.

69 AI Agents. Specialized agents for tutoring, code review, writing, research, fleet management, security auditing, and more. Each agent has domain expertise and persistent memory. They coordinate through NATS pub/sub messaging.

Privacy First. Your data never leaves your network. No cloud training, no data harvesting, no surveillance. You own everything. The entire system runs on WireGuard-encrypted mesh networking between your own devices.
